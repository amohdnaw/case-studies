I audited my own trading system — and killed my best result.

Five months of paper trades showed +0.45% per trade. The number was real, reproducible, and meaningless. This is a study in running the statistics that hurt.

Same-day signals on correlated assets are one bet, not thirty. The honest sample is 89 days, not 1,555 trades.
Verdict — no demonstrable edge. Real-trade net expectancy +0.447%/trade, but the day-clustered 95% CI is [−0.25, +1.08] — it includes zero. Gross accuracy 53.2% against a 55.2% up-move base rate.
